Lasagna


Description

To make lasagna, start by preparing a rich meat sauce with ground beef, onions, garlic, and your favorite tomato sauce, simmering until the flavors meld together. Simultaneously, mix ricotta cheese with beaten eggs, grated Parmesan, chopped parsley, and a hint of nutmeg. Boil lasagna noodles until they are al dente, then lay them out flat to avoid sticking. In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles. Over the noodles, spread a portion of the ricotta mixture, then sprinkle generously with shredded mozzarella. Repeat the layering process until all ingredients are used, ending with a final layer of noodles topped with meat sauce and a thick layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover the dish with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F for about 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown. Allow the lasagna to rest for about 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving, ensuring each piece is perfectly layered and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • garlic
  • Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Ground Beef

Recipe

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare the meat sauce by sautéing chopped onions and garlic in olive oil until they are soft. Add ground beef or sausage, cooking until browned. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, and seasonings. Let it simmer for at least 30 minutes.
  3. While the sauce simmers, cook the lasagna noodles in a large pot of boiling salted water until they are al dente. Drain and lay them out flat on a clean surface to prevent sticking.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with beaten eggs, grated Parmesan, chopped parsley, and a pinch of nutmeg. Mix until well blended.
  5. In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  6. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  7. Spread a portion of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  8. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta mixture.
  9. Repeat the layering process: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and a final topping of mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, making sure it doesn't touch the cheese to avoid sticking.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  13. Let the lasagna rest for about 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
